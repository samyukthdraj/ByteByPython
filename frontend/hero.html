About

"CAP: Bridging Communities and Authorities through AI." CAP leverages cutting-edge AI technology to revolutionize crime detection and prevention. By enabling civilians to actively contribute to public safety. CAP transforms traditional methods of law enforcement into a collaborative effort between the public and authorities.

With the power of artificial intelligence, CAP processes images, videos, and voice inputs uploaded by users, automatically analyzing and detecting potential criminal activities. This not only speeds up investigations but also ensures that no valuable information is overlooked.
